Likas

Likas is an eco-conscious mobile app that feels like TikTok, but built around climate action and sustainable living. Users can scroll through short-form eco reels, read community blogs, and discover local green initiatives all in one feed. The experience is designed to make learning about the environment feel fun, social, and highly shareable.

The app also integrates EcoMarket, a curated space for sustainable products and services, so users can move seamlessly from awareness to action. The current prototype is designed in Figma with a mobile-first UI, content discovery sections for EcoTalks (blogs), EcoReels (short videos), and EcoMarket, and is being prepared for deployment as a full interactive app.
